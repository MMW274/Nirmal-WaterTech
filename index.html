Who we are

Ultimate solution providers
for environment protection

"Providing value to customers is our main objective. We strive to build our market leadership in water and wastewater treatment, and contribute to cleaner and greener environments."

Nirmal Water Tech is established to provide solutions in water and wastewater treatment plants and services. We are in the business of design, supply, erection, and commissioning of water and wastewater treatment plants — inclusive of physical, chemical, and biological methods — for handling wastewater from various industries and domestic drinking water.

We provide turnkey solutions including Engineering, Procurement & Construction of water treatment plants for industrial and institutional applications: Softeners, DM/DI plants, Filtration, RO, MBR, MBBR, UV purifiers, Ozonators, and the complete range of Effluent and Sewage Treatment Plants. Upgradation and augmentation of existing plants is our proven speciality.

Our experts carry rich experience in EC (Environment Clearance) — a critical requirement under Central and State Government guidelines — covering Design and Drawing services for WTPs and STPs.

Design & Engineering
We conceptualise, design, develop, and manufacture the complete range of water and wastewater treatment plants with allied equipment.
Turnkey SITC
Supply, Installation, Testing, and Commissioning across the nation. One team handles the full project from site survey to handover.
Retrofitting & Augmentation
Upgrading existing plants to current Pollution Control Board norms is our proven identity, built over many years of industrial work.
Spares & Consumables
We also supply water treatment plant spares, consumables, chemicals, and ion exchange resins.

Zero Liquid Discharge

Eliminate discharge.
Recover your water.

ZLD systems treat your most difficult high-TDS wastewater streams through evaporation until no liquid discharge remains. The recovered water returns to your process.

With tightening ZLD mandates from India's Pollution Control Boards — particularly for textile, sugar, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries — we help clients achieve full compliance while recovering water for reuse.
